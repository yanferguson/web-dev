Creamy Ditalini Pasta

  1. Getting Started

    • Heat up pan
    • Add butter (however much you'd like)
    • Add 1 tbsp of minced garlic

  2. Cooking the Noodles

    • Add 4 cups of chicken broth to the pan
    • Next add 1 cup of ditaline or penne pasta to the pan
    • Let it simmer with the lid on medium heat for 10-12 minutes

  3. After the Patsa is Done

    • Strain chicken broth (keep a little)
    • Add 1 tbsp of melted butter to noodles
    • Add 1/4 cup of heavy whipping cream to noodles
    • Add 1/4 tbsp of salt
    • Add 1/4 tbsp of pepper
    • Add 1/2 cup of mozzerella cheese and let melt

  4. After Mixing

    • Let sit for 3 minutes
    • Enjoy!
