Top Pop-Punk Bands

Hey! As an upcoming artist in the pop punk/punk rock world, it's very important to remember and hold on to your influences. In the text below, I ranked the top pop-punk bands and listed their discographies so you can check out how they've grown over the years.

Green Day

I could talk about Green Day all day long and never get bored of it. They are what introduced me into the pop-punk realm. They're influences were bands like The Ramones and The Replacements which is what inspires part of their sound. They orioritize guitar, bass, and drums which is what makes them such a great band. Billie Joe Armstrong (frontman) uses amazing techniques that are simple yet so powerful. They started out in 1988 and have been making music since then with 17 albums and over 200 (including records and the opposite sides its over 400) which is honestly so impressive. Their top song was "Basket Case" with 1.49 BILLION streams. And their top Album is American Idiot which was released in 2004. Before releasing their hit album, their previous album, Warning,was a flop, but they made up for it. Personally, Warning is one of my favorite albums yet because it includes acoustic guitar which holds a special place in my heart.
